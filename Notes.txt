
Rules:-
1. always first design for mobile then go for larger screens
2. add styling in class



h-screen:- 100% view port height (i.e height of the screen)

w-full:- 100% width of the screen

mx-auto:- iska matlab 80% height and width lega upna parent ka; agar parent ka size ghatega to iska bi size ghatega ( use hota hai responsive karne keliya)

px-4:- iska matlab padding from left and right 4 px

py-3:- iska matlab padding from top and bottom 3 px

agar apna man se kuch dena chahateho then uss chiz ko square bracket ke andar likho
eg:- font-['gilroy'] or bg-[#hexcode values]

hidden:- isko likh ne se content hidden hota hai

lg:block :- isko likh ne se jo content hidden hua tha large screen par dikhaega

mt-20:- margin-top 

items-center:- to bring center

tracking-tight:- letter spacing
